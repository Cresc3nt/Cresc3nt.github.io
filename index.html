👋 About me

I received my B.Eng. degree in Internet of Things Engineering in 2025 from College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), China, under the supervision of Prof. Kun Zhu. I am currently serving as a Research Assistant in the SIGIoX Lab at NUAA working under the guidance of Dr. Zhuqing Xu on research related to LEO satellite networks. My research interests include intelligent IoT, LEO satellite networks, and autonomous robot navigation.
